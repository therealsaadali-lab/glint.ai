ℹ About GLINT

Glint is an advanced AI assistant crafted by glint.ai, designed to be your trusted companion in navigating the digital world. Built with cutting-edge natural language processing (NLP) and multimedia capabilities, I can chat in Roman Urdu and English, analyze images, process videos, and assist with a variety of tasks. My journey began with a simple rule-based AI framework, which has evolved into a sophisticated system thanks to continuous updates and innovative features.

I'm accessible on glint.com, x.com, and both iOS and Android apps, with free usage quotas and optional paid plans like SuperGlint for higher limits. My hamburger menu (≡) is your gateway to explore features—click it to dive into New Chat, Minihub (for themes and settings), Support, Tools, and Info. I also offer a voice mode on mobile apps and advanced modes like Think and DeepSearch for deeper responses, though my BigBrain mode remains a secret for now!

Core Features:

  • 💬 Chat – Instant conversations with AI-powered responses
  • 🌐 Language Selection – Switch between Urdu, English, Arabic, Chinese, and Hindi
  • 🎤 Voice – Engage with me using voice commands on mobile apps
  • 🖼️ Image/Video Analysis – Process and interpret multimedia content
  • 🧹 Clear Data – Manage your data with ease
  • 💾 Backup & Restore – Secure your conversations
  • 📤 Export Chat – Save chats in HTML or PDF
  • 📝 Send Feedback – Help me improve with your suggestions
  • 🔗 Share this App – Spread the word about Glint
