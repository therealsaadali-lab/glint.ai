1. Introduction

Welcome to GLINT, an AI-powered assistant developed by AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled with the utmost care. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our services, accessible via glint.com, the GLINT iOS/Android apps, or other platforms. GLINT is designed to assist users globally with features like Roman Urdu support, image processing, and voice mode.

2. Information We Collect
  • Device Information: We collect basic data such as browser type, language preference (e.g., Roman Urdu), and operating system to optimize your experience.
  • Chat History: Your conversation history is stored locally on your device to maintain continuity across sessions, including multilingual chats.
  • Optional Data: You may choose to share additional data via the GLINT API, voice input, or uploaded images/videos, which enhances functionality like AI-generated responses.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data to improve our services and user experience.
3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI services, including voice mode and image analysis.
  • Personalize your experience with customizable themes and language preferences.
  • Ensure the security and functionality of our platform across all access points (web, app).
  • Communicate updates, promotions, or support messages related to GLINT features.
  • Conduct research and development to enhance AI capabilities.
4. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Data is stored securely on your device or encrypted servers when necessary (e.g., for API processing). Voice data and uploaded media are handled with end-to-end encryption where applicable. We implement regular security audits and comply with industry standards for data protection.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared with trusted third parties only when required to:

  • Provide core functionalities (e.g., AI API integrations for GLINT's AI capabilities).
  • Comply with legal obligations or protect user safety and rights.
  • Facilitate business transfers, mergers, or acquisitions.
  • Conduct academic research with anonymized data.
6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data stored locally or via the app.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection (e.g., voice or API usage).
  • Request clarification on how your data is used by contacting support.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Object to specific data processing activities.
7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, analyze traffic within GLINT, and enhance your overall experience. These include essential cookies for core functionality, analytics cookies for service improvement, and preference cookies for personalization. You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings or app settings. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed toward children under the age of 13 (or the age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children and will delete it immediately if identified. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal data should contact us immediately for removal.

9. Third-Party Services

Our services may link to third-party platforms. Please review their respective privacy policies as we are not responsible for their practices. We carefully vet third-party services for compliance with privacy standards but cannot guarantee their practices.

10. International Data Transfers

If you access GLINT outside your home country, your data may be transferred across borders (e.g., for AI API processing). We ensure compliance with applicable data protection regulations like GDPR and CCPA during such transfers through standard contractual clauses, adequacy decisions, or other approved mechanisms.

11. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect new features (e.g., voice mode enhancements) or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date will reflect the latest version. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notifications or email. Continued use of GLINT after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Legal Compliance

We may disclose information if legally required, such as responding to court orders, legal processes, or government requests, to ensure compliance with applicable laws, including those affecting AI technologies. We will notify users of such requests when legally permitted.

13. Retention of Data

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined (e.g., chat history for session continuity) or as required by law. Data stored locally remains until you choose to delete it via the "Clear Data" option. Server-side data is automatically purged after 30 days of inactivity.

14. Limitations of Liability

While we take every precaution to safeguard your data, we cannot guarantee complete security against all risks. By using GLINT, you acknowledge these limitations and agree that we are not liable for unauthorized access beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to hacking, phishing, or other cybersecurity breaches.

15. Consent

By using GLINT, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. You can withdraw consent by clearing data or discontinuing use of the app. Withdrawal of consent may limit your access to certain features that require data processing.
